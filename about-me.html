Biography

London based British musician, Nick Lennon, has been bringing his passion for drumming, performance and entertainment to stage for over 10 years. His technical abilities are the culmination of drum lessons from the age of 8, achieving a degree in Contemporary Music Performance and private study with many prestigious tutors including Ed Spevock and Pete Riley.

Nick is a very adaptable player who has a broad spectrum of percussive skills including tuned, auxiliary and electronic percussion.

With his infectious stage presence, interminable energy and drumming prowess, every performance of Nick's is a joy to watch and listen to.

As a drum kit tutor, Nick encourages and pushes his students to reach their full drumming potential, through individually designed learning plans, drawing on his experience working in the music industry to make the lessons relevant.

Career Highlights

2016- 2020

Performed on Cruise Ships

Nick spent 5 years performing on cruise ships all over the world in many roles from drummer in the jazz band, and Royal Court Theatre Drummer to Musical Director. During this time, travelling the world and visiting over 50 countries, Nick worked for Cunard, P&O Australia, P&O UK and SAGA Cruises Ltd and has worked on over 6 different ships.

2014

Graduated from University

Nick graduated from The Academy of Contemporary Music in late 2014 after completing a Higher Diploma and Degree in Contemporary Music Performance.

2012

London 2012 Olympic Ceremonies

Nick was lucky enough to work as part of the team involved in putting together a section of the London 2012 Olympic Opening Ceremony. As part of this performance, he joined 1000 other drummers in the Pandemonium section, which was directed by Danny Boyle. Throughout the rehearsals, which lasted for several months, and performances Nick bumped into many famous faces in an experience he would never forget.
